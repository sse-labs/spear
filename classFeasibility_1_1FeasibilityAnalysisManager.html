Detailed Description

FeasibilityAnalysisManager is the central component for managing the state of the feasibility analysis, including the storage of formulas (as sets of atomic formulas) and environments (variable bindings). It provides methods for interning formula sets, extending environments, and applying PHI node effects on environments. The manager also holds the Z3 context and solver used for formula manipulation and satisfiability checking.
